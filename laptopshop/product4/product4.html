ThinkPad X1 Carbon Gen 10 (14” Intel) Laptop

October marks 30 years of ThinkPad and we’re commemorating this monumental occasion with a special, limited run of ThinkPad X1 Carbon 30th Anniversary Edition laptops! The special edition includes the classic ThinkPad logo in red, green, and blue (RGB). What’s more, it’s branded with a 30th Anniversary Edition etching just below the keyboard. Plus, commemorative serial numbers are engraved on the right hinge cap, starting at 0001.
